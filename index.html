Founded in 2008 by Bobby Mancini, Bobby’s Pizza was born from a lifelong love of traditional Italian cuisine and a dream of bringing authentic, wood-fired pizza to the heart of the community. From humble beginnings in a cozy corner shop, Bobby’s commitment to quality and flavor quickly earned the pizzeria a loyal following.

Using the freshest local ingredients, we honor classic recipes, and serve every pizza with heart. Each pie that leaves our oven is hand-crafted, stone-baked, and infused with the passion that has defined us from day one.

Over the years, Bobby’s Pizza has grown into a neighborhood staple known for its welcoming atmosphere, friendly service, and unmistakable aroma of dough, sauce, and melted mozzarella. Whether you’re grabbing a quick slice or sitting down for a family dinner, our team is here to make you feel right at home — one delicious bite at a time.
